Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BudgetFaye

Can I use the free plan forever?

Yes, our free plan is available indefinitely with all the features listed. You can upgrade to Premium anytime for additional capabilities.

How does the AI analyze my invoices?

Our AI uses machine learning to extract key information from your invoices, categorize expenses, detect anomalies, and provide insights into your spending patterns.

Can I switch between Free and Premium?

Absolutely! You can upgrade to Premium at any time, and if you decide it's not for you, you can downgrade back to Free at the end of your billing cycle.

Is my data secure with BudgetFaye?

Yes, we take security seriously. All data is encrypted, and we comply with industry-standard security protocols to keep your information safe.
